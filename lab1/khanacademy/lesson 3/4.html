Recipe #1: Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground Beef 500g
Tomato Sauce 2 cups
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Step 1: Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
Step 2: In a separate pan, cook the ground beef with onions and garlic.
Step 3: Add the tomato sauce to the beef mixture and simmer for 20 minutes.
Step 4: Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and garnish with parmesan.

Recipe #2: Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 2 pieces
Mixed Greens 4 cups
Avocado 1, sliced
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Lemon 1, squeezed
Step 1: Grill the chicken breasts until fully cooked.
Step 2: Toss the mixed greens and avocado with olive oil and lemon juice.
Step 3: Slice the grilled chicken and place on top of the salad.
Step 4: Serve and enjoy!
